Introduction: You will complete a set of puzzles. On the screen, you will see a set of abstract pictures with one piece missing. Among a set of possible choices, you must select which one completes the pattern. For example, in the figure below, choice B completes the pattern and would be the correct answer.

*Figure*

You will have 8 minutes to complete 12 such puzzles. A timer will be shown in the upper righthand corner as you complete the puzzles.

Payment: After the 8 minutes allocated for this part, time will end. At the end of the experiment, the computer will randomly choose 2 puzzles from the 12. Each puzzle has the same chance of being chosen. For each of these two puzzles you solved correctly, you will earn 100LD.
